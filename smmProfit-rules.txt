How the New Numbers Play Out:

On a cheap ₦200 package: 20% of ₦200 is only ₦40. With the new rule, 
the system skips that entirely and injects the ₦150 minimum profit,
making the final selling price ₦350. You actually make a meaningful margin here.

On a mid-range ₦1,000 package: 20% of ₦1,000 is ₦200. 
Since ₦200 is safely above your new ₦150 floor, it applies the exact 20% rate,
making the selling price ₦1,200.

On a massive ₦10,000 bulk package: 20% of ₦10,000 would be ₦2,000.
The new ₦1,500 cap steps in to keep your prices highly competitive for bulk buyers,
making the final selling price ₦11,500 while still landing you a clean ₦1,500 profit
on a single transaction.
